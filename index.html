There is so much good to be done

You already have the passion - we give you the business skills and hands-on experience to make a difference. Whether you're transforming ideas into profitable, social, and sustainable business ventures or you're influencing change within an organization, "IMPACT MBA" helps you get there.

Make the Most Impact

When you join this program, you join of the best and most impactful social enterprise programs in the U.S. You'll learn how to create and operate ventures that have the power to find solutions to the most complex global challenges, and apply market-based approaches to causes that matter to you. Whether you're drive to make your entrepreneurial mark or drive change within an existing organization, the Impact MBA provides the practical skill set you need.

Experience That Matters

The centerpiece of theis unique, 16-month program is our summer practicum in which student teams develop enterprise solutions and travel to the field to apply what they've learned in the classroom.

The 40-60 day field study is an intense hands-on experience where you'll develop the enhanced leadership, teamwork and business skills necessary to identify and create opportunities where the markets fall short. You'll graduate prepared to build and manage global ventures that achieve sustainability with results focused on the triple bottom line of economic, social, and environmental performance.

Take the best of the experience you already have and prepare for whatever comes next. Get started by requesting information using the form above.

Business + Sustainability Across CSU

This interdisciplinary degree allows you to pursue your passions across Colorado State University. Extend the core business curriculum with the Venture Creation track, designed to help you make your mark in the market, or the Corporate Sustainability track, designed to help you be a change agent within an organization.

Qualify for In-State Tuition

If you live in the Wester U.S. and are considering our program in sunny Colorado, you may also qualify for in-state tuition through the Western Interstate Commission for Higher Education (WICHE). We also have graduate assistships, scholarships, fellowships and more to offset the cost of tuition.
